Review

Article 370 serves as a persuasive explainer on the Indian government’s Kashmir policy leading to the abrogation of the region's special status on August 5, 2019. Timed around election season, the film feels like a strategic move, presenting the decision as a masterstroke.

While the film highlights the business of terrorism and the outdated methods of back-channel diplomacy, it leans heavily into the official narrative, often vilifying local Kashmiri leaders while avoiding Delhi’s deeper role in the conflict.
